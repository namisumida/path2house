Past experiences of House members

The new House of Representatives has been noted as being more female, representative, and novice in their political carerrs. This tool examines the different past experiences of House members including education, prior careers, and previously-held political offices. Some experiences - like attending law school or working in business - are more common than others - like receiving a doctorate or pursuing a career in sports. And some overlap heavily with other experiences: xx tended to go into xx.

Data and inspiration for this story come from this visualization by The New York Times, as I felt that I could create an interactive visualization that allows for more exploration and comparison than what is available in the NYT visualization.
